# MTB-Track-Timer

MTB time-tracking system powered by a Heltec LoRa V3. Designed to measure ride times, split laps, and test performance on your favorite trails. Built for mountain bikers who love data, DIY, and a bit of tech adventure!


How to use: 

- Flash Node 1 with *Node-1.ino
- Flash Node 2 with *Node-2.ino 
- Start both Devices and Place them on your trials
- Check out your WIFI Settings and Connect on off Devices 
- Open your Browser and go to http://192.168.4.1


Requirements:

- 2x Heltec V3 
- 2x NO Button and wires
- 2x Case (Model for 3D Print in progress)
